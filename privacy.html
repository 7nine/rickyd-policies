Ricky D Assistant — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Overview

This service is a personal AI assistant that sends SMS notifications to the account owner and designated recipients only.

Consent & Opt-In

By configuring RickyD and providing your phone number, you expressly consent to receive automated notifications via SMS. You may revoke consent at any time by replying STOP to any message. Only the account owner who configures the system will receive messages. No third-party numbers are added without direct owner action.

Information Collection

We collect phone numbers solely for the purpose of delivering SMS notifications.

Data Security

Phone numbers are stored securely and never shared with third parties.

Data Usage

No data is sold or used for marketing purposes.

Message Delivery

Messages are sent via Twilio's messaging platform.

Opting Out

To stop receiving messages, reply STOP at any time.

Contact
